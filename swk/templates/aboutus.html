{% trans "About Swachh Worli Koliwada" %}


{% trans "Background" %}

{% blocktrans %}IDFC FIRST Bank in collaboration with Municipal Corporation of Greater Mumbai (MCGM) and Stree Mukti Sanghatana spearheaded Swachh Worli Koliwada project with an aim to create awareness and bring about a behavioural change in the community on solid waste management practices and adoption of waste segregation at source. The project plans to drive this change through a participatory process within the Worli Koliwada community. {% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %}Swachh Worli Koliwada project aims at building a zero waste neighbourhood with self-sustainable zones that are equipped to manage solid waste they generate, with a model where community participation will be a key at every stage, ensuring sustainability of this program. {% endblocktrans %}

{% trans "About Stree Mukti Sangathana(SMS)" %}

{% blocktrans %}Stree Mukti Sanghatana established in 1975 is a non-profit organisation and strives to create a gender just world by improving the lives of women through education, awareness, healthcare, and opportunities for livelihood. Its vision is eradication of poverty and violence, and creation of a conducive atmosphere for equality. It engages in the process of dialogues and reformation through various activities like Theatre, in-house monthly publication “Prerak Lalkari”, Family Counselling centres, Adolescent sensitization program, creating sustainable livelihood programmes etc. {% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %}Since 1999 SMS has been working for the cause of women waste pickers and offering solutions for Solid Waste Management. SMS is registered under Bombay Public Trusts Act and Societies registration act and has 80-G and 12 A certificates under the Income Tax Act, 1961. {% endblocktrans %}
